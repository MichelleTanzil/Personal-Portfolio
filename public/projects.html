A calendar built based of Django’s HTMLCalendar. The end user can add, edit, and delete events on this calendar. The calendar allows the user to navigate to the next and previous month, along with a yearly view to navigate to the specific month and year indicated. The calendar can also switch between a light and dark view. This project was completed in 5 days.

An 8-bit platform game based off Unity2D’s game engine and C# language. The player can run, jump, be killed by enemies and/or hazards, and kill enemies. The player can also pause the game, restart the game, and navigate back to the start menu. A total of 3 levels with increasing difficulty was made. Learning Unity and the project was completed in 9 days.
